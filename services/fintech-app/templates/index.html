Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started without a bank account?

Simply sign up with your phone number. No bank account or ID verification is required to get started.

What are the transaction fees?

Fees vary by plan. Starter users pay standard fees, while Pro and Advanced members enjoy reduced rates.

Is my money safe?

We use industry-leading security measures including encryption, multi-sig wallets, and DAO-governed insurance.

How does DAO governance work?

Token holders vote on platform decisions, ensuring community-driven development and transparent operations.

Can I withdraw back to mobile money?

Yes! Convert your crypto back to mobile money instantly and withdraw to your registered phone number.

What mobile money services are supported?

We support M-Pesa (Kenya), MTN (Ghana/Nigeria), Airtel Money, and other major providers.
